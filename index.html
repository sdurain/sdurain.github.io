Braking pad semantic segmentation

Computer Vision · Segmentation · Materials
Semantic segmentation
  • Goal: Perform semantic segmentation of brake pads to quantify tribological phenomena.
  • Preprocessing: Generate initial semantic masks via clustering-based segmentation of SEM images, seeded by superpixels.
  • Training: Fine-tune (transfer learning) a SegFormer model, monitored using mIoU and Dice metrics.
  • Outputs: Quantitative monitoring of the brake-pad contact interface.
